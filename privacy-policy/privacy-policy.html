Lanebreak Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Lanebreak ("we", "our", or "us") provides a swimming training and analysis application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using Lanebreak, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Health and Fitness Data (Apple Health / HealthKit)

With your permission, Lanebreak reads workout and activity information from Apple Health (HealthKit). This may include:

  • Swim workout duration
  • Distance swum
  • Lap counts
  • Stroke type
  • Heart rate
  • Calories burned
  • Timestamps of lengths
  • Other swimming workout metrics recorded by Apple Watch

We only access data that you explicitly authorize in Apple Health permissions. We do not write data to Apple Health.

b. Account Information

We additionally collect:

  • Email address (if an account is created)
  • Subscription status
  • App preferences (such as swim goals)

c. Usage Information

We collect limited technical data necessary to operate the app, such as:

  • App version
  • Device type
  • Crash logs
  • Feature usage (analytics)

This data does not include personal health metrics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data only to operate and improve Lanebreak, including:

  • Analyzing your swim workouts
  • Generating coaching insights
  • Calculating training metrics
  • Showing progress trends
  • Syncing your workouts across devices
  • Troubleshooting and improving the app

3. Health Data Protections

HealthKit data is treated with special care.

We do not:

  • Sell health data
  • Share health data with advertisers
  • Use health data for marketing
  • Use health data for profiling
  • Share health data with data brokers

Your HealthKit data is used only to provide swim coaching and analytics features inside the app.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal or health data.

We may share limited non-health information only with service providers that help us operate the app, such as:

  • Secure cloud hosting
  • Subscription processing
  • Crash reporting

These providers may only use data to perform services for us.

5. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to safeguard your data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You can control your data at any time:

Apple Health Permissions

You may revoke access at any time in:

iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Lanebreak

Delete Your Data

You may request deletion of your account and stored data by contacting:

nigina@alpivie.com

7. Children's Privacy

Lanebreak is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
